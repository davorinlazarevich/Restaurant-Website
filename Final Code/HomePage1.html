Our History

Pizza Place is a small, family owned business, that began with one store located in Chicago City,in 2001. Pizza Place has always been
focused on quality since day one in our very first store. We have always believed that you deserve our very best. By utilizing the freshest
and highest quality ingredients and producing our own signature sauce and pizza dough in our place. we are able to deliver that award
winning pizza to your home or table every time. Word of mouth spreads fast, especially about pizza here in Chicago!
